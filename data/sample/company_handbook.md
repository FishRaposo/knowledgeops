# Acme Corp Company Handbook

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Corporation! This handbook contains essential information about our company policies, procedures, and culture. We're thrilled to have you on board.

## Company Overview

Acme Corporation was founded in 2010 and specializes in enterprise software solutions. We have offices in San Francisco, New York, and London with over 500 employees worldwide.

### Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower organizations through innovative technology solutions that simplify complex workflows and drive measurable business outcomes.

## Office Policies

### Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible working arrangements are available with manager approval. Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM when all team members should be available.

### Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requires a stable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps) and a dedicated workspace. All remote workers must be available during core hours.

### Dress Code

Business casual is the standard dress code. On client-facing days, professional attire is expected. Casual Fridays allow jeans and t-shirts.

## Benefits

### Health Insurance

Acme provides comprehensive health insurance covering medical, dental, and vision. Coverage begins on the first day of employment. Family coverage is available at a subsidized rate.

### Vacation

- **0-2 years:** 15 days paid time off
- **2-5 years:** 20 days paid time off
- **5+ years:** 25 days paid time off

Unused vacation days roll over up to 5 days per year.

### Retirement

Acme offers a 401(k) plan with 4% employer match. Vesting occurs after 2 years of employment.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior, respect colleagues, and adhere to ethical business practices. Violations should be reported to HR immediately.

### Anti-Harassment Policy

Acme has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Reports can be made anonymously through our ethics hotline at 1-800-ACME-ETHICS.

## Technology

### Equipment

All employees receive a company laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell Latitude) and necessary peripherals. Equipment must be returned upon separation.

### Software

Approved software includes Slack, Google Workspace, Jira, Confluence, and GitHub. Additional software requests should go through IT support.
