# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2024

## Overview

At Acme Corporation, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which we offer refunds for our products and services.

## Eligibility

### Software Subscriptions

- Refunds are available within **14 days** of the initial purchase for new subscriptions
- Renewals are non-refundable unless cancelled before the renewal date
- Annual plans can be refunded on a prorated basis within 30 days of purchase

### One-Time Purchases

- Digital products (templates, guides) are non-refundable once downloaded
- Physical products can be returned within **30 days** of delivery in original packaging

## How to Request a Refund

1. Log into your Acme account at account.acme.com
2. Navigate to **Billing > Purchase History**
3. Select the purchase you wish to refund
4. Click **Request Refund** and provide a reason
5. Our team will review and respond within **3 business days**

## Refund Processing

- Refunds are processed to the original payment method
- Credit card refunds appear within **5-10 business days**
- Bank transfer refunds appear within **7-14 business days**
- Refund amounts are calculated in the original purchase currency

## Exceptions

The following are not eligible for refunds:
- Services already rendered (consulting, training)
- Custom development work
- Third-party integrations configured on your behalf
- Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
